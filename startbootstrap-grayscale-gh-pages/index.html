Our Scandinavian ancestors travelled north. They discovered a landscape of forests and sandy beaches, with bright and mild summers but harsh winters. They learnt modesty, to save valuable resources and to always rely on their common sense. We are all modern-day nomads, the world is our home. In this mix of cultures, we find peace in the uncomplicated and joy in the simple and functional. While Norrmade is looking to the world, our roots are solidly planted in the Nordic soil.
